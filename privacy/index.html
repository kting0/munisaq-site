隱私權政策 Privacy Policy
1. 您的基本權利 Your basic rights

我們致力於提供你完全透明、合理、即時且易懂的隱私權政策。

We are committed to fair and full transparency in privacy policies and will continue to provide up-to-date information in a way that people can easily understand.

2. 個資定義與個資流通目的地 Definition and circulation destinations of personal data

你的個資可能包含搜尋時間、搜尋內容、搜尋次數、語言設定、最愛車站、IP位址、支持者付款紀錄。這些資訊部分會儲存在裝置中，部分可能透過Munisaq經由網路上傳到（一）臺灣交通部運輸資料流通服務，與（二）Apple公司。

Your personal data may include search time, search content, search count, language settings, favorite stations, IP address, supporter payment records. The data might be saved in your device, and some might be uploaded to the internet to (1) Transport Data Exchange Service provided by the Taiwanese Ministry of Transportation and Communications, and (2) Apple Inc.

3. 交通部對個資的收集與處理 Ministry of Transportation and Communications' collection and process of your personal data

你必須同意交通部運輸資料流通服務平臺的隱私權保護政策才能使用Munisaq。該政策指出你的IP位址、搜尋紀錄與其他個資可能被交通部收集並處理，但未有特殊情況不會將個資轉讓給其他單位或組織，僅限平臺內部統計使用。

You have to agree to the privacy policy of Transport Data Exchange Service provided by the Taiwanese Transportation Ministry before you use Munisaq. The policy indicates that your IP address, search history and other personal data might be collected and processed by the Ministry of Transportation and Communications. But they will only use your data for internal analysis and won't transfer your data to other departments or organizations except under special circumstances.

4. Apple對個資的收集與處理 Apple's collection and process of your personal data

你的Munisaq使用紀錄、IP位址、支持者付款紀錄與其他個資可能會被Apple公司收集與處理，且此關係受於你與Apple公司於Munisaq外訂立之協議管轄。

Your Munisaq usage records, IP address, supporter payment records might be collected and processed by Apple Inc. This relationship is regulated by your agreements with Apple Inc. outside Munisaq.

5. 開發者使用裝置上個資 Developer usage of personal data on device

未經你明確同意，Munisaq開發者無法檢視你儲存於裝置的個資，所有對於你的個資處理都不會經過開發者伺服器。

Without your explicit consent, the developer of Munisaq cannot view your personal data on device. Your personal data doesn't go through the developer's severs.

6. 第三方對開發者提供的個資 Personal data provided to developer by third parties

基於你與交通部和Apple公司的協議，這兩個單位可能會將Munisaq相關之統計資訊、使用紀錄與其他個資提供給Munisaq開發者。這些資料僅供內部統計與服務使用，不會提供、更不會轉售給其他單位。

Based on your agreements with the Ministry of Transportation and Communications and Apple Inc., these organizations might provide stastitics, usage data and other personal data related to Munisaq to the developer. The data will be used for service and internal analysis and won't be provided or sold to other organizations.

7. 開發者的義務 Developer's obligations

Munisaq開發者有義務回應使用者對此隱私權政策相關的合理提問與要求，且若此隱私權政策改變對使用者有重大影響，開發者需於軟體醒目處提醒。

The developer is obliged to react to reasonable questions and demands from users regarding this privacy policy. If any changes of this privacy policy impacts users significantly, the developer has to remind the user in a noticeable place on Munisaq.
