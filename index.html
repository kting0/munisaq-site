Munisaq

Munisaq是2022年開始於App Store提供iPhone使用者下載的臺鐵時刻表軟體，也是布農語「去哪裡」的意思。Munisaq希望藉由嚴格的隱私權政策與提供完整的臺鐵時刻查詢功能，讓使用者享受自在、隱私優先、沒有第三方廣告的旅行體驗。

Munisaq is a Timetable app for Taiwan Railways avaliable on the App Store for iPhone users since 2022. It means “where to” in the Bunun language. Munisaq aims to offer seamless, privacy-first travel experience without third party ads by providing comprehensive rail information and a strict privacy policy.
